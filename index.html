Elon Musk

Timeline

  • June 28,1971 - Born to engineer father and model mother in South Africa
  • Age 8 - Runs out of books to read at library, reads entire Encyclopedia Britannica
  • Age 9 - Parents divorce, chooses to live with father
  • Age 12 - Creates and writes a video game called Blastar, sells it for $500
  • Age 14 - Has existential crisis -- reads 'The Hitcherkiler's Guide to The Galaxy'; looking for an answer
  • Age 17 - Moves to Canada; before that he graduates from Pretoria Boys High School with distinctions in Science and Computer Studies
  • Age 18 - Attends college at Queen's University in Ontario. Then transfers to the UPenn, completed a BS in Economics (Wharton) and a BA with a major in physics in 1994
  • 1995 - Moves to Sillicon Valley; defers graduate program in applied physics and material science at Stanford University
  • February 1999 - Sells Zip2 to Compaq for $307 million, of which $22 million went to musk. Then forms X.com, which later in 2000 morphs into PayPal.
  • July 2002 - eBay acquires PayPal for $1.5 billion is stock, of which $165 million goes to musk
  • 2004 - Invests in Tesla
  • August 2006 - Becomes first investor in SolarCity, his cousin company
  • March 2008 - First Tesla customers get their Roadsters at $109,000
  • March 2009 - Unveils the first Tesla Model S
  • 2011 - Promises to send humans to Mars by 2021-2031
  • June 2012 - Starts shipping Tesla Model S
  • September 27, 2016 - Unveils plan to colonise Mars
  • July,2020 - Spacex succeds with sending and returning two astronauts, bob and doug to nasa spacial station.
